# digital-writing-folio

_**What types of writing do you do (e.g. fiction, nonfiction, poetry)?**_

I began my writing journey with writing flash fiction for youth story competitions and then as I grew older, I began writing poetry. While that has been my primary form, I have experimented with creative non-ficiton pieces and really enjoyed that form. So, I would say that I'm fluid with my writing.

_**What tools (or techniques) do you use to write? Why do you use them?**_

When I'm scribbling--what I call the work I produce that is less serious--I either use my notes app or a small notebook that I keep with me. Taking writing less seriously allows me to combat my perfectionism and in doing so, helps me produce more and produce better. When I'm writing seriously--for an assignment or a project that I'm submitting somewhere--I use a Word Document and write with the Comic Sans font in order for me to distinguish between drafting and editing (in which I change the font to Times New Roman).
